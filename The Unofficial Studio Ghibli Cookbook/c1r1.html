Skillet Bacon and Eggs

Inspired by a magical castle, a spiteful witch, and a fire demon.

This recipe is inspired by one of my favorite Studio Ghibli films about a spiteful witch, a curse, and a moving castle. You’ll have to cook it without the help of your favorite fire demon, but it’ll be a delicious and mouthwatering breakfast all the same. Make sure to use thick-cut bacon so you get as much grease as possible. I like to use toucinho (thick cuts of Portuguese-style smoked bacon). Serve with a hot bowl of tea, and you’ll feel like you’ve just woken up in a magical castle!

Yield: 2 to 4 servings
Prep time: 0 minutes
Cook time: 20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp neutral oil (e.g., canola)
  • 7 oz thick-cut bacon or toucinho
  • 4 large eggs
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 4 cups water
  • 2-4 tsp Jasmine tea leaves or any tea of your choice
  • Loaf of ciabatta or any crusty bread of choice, sliced
  • 3.5 oz Maasdam cheese or any cheese of choice

✨Ghibli-style breakfast like Howl's!

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a large cast-iron pan over medium heat.
  2. Add the bacon to the pan and fry until it is crispy on both slides, about 5 to 10 minutes.
  3. Move the bacon to the sides of the pan. Crack the eggs directly into the same pan and cook until the egg whites are set, about 3 to 4 minutes. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. In an electric kettle or a small saucepan, heat the water to a boil.
  5. Add 1 teaspoon of tea leaves and the boiling water to each bowl (use 2 to 4 bowls, depending how many people you are feeding). Let the tea steep for 3 minutes.
  6. Serve all the bacon and eggs family style (in one pan) accompanied with the crusty slices of bread, a hunk of Maasdam cheese or your cheese of choice, and bowl of green tea. This meal can be shared among a small family.

✨ Enjoy this cozy breakfast like you just woke up in a magical moving castle!
